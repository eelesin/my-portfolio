About Me
"I don't just administer platforms — I architect the systems that make enterprises move faster, safer and smarter."

I am a Senior Platform Engineer with over 10 years of experience owning and scaling enterprise systems that organisations depend on. My work sits at the intersection of identity, automation and cloud infrastructure, connecting platforms, eliminating manual processes and building governance frameworks that survive audits and scale with the business.

At Riot Games I led the end to end Server-to-Cloud migration for 25,000 users, delivering on time with zero data loss. At HITRUST I owned the entire Atlassian and NetSuite ecosystem as the sole platform engineer, operating in one of the most compliance-intensive environments in the industry. Across both organisations I designed and delivered automated incident pipelines, vulnerability management systems, REST API integrations and identity architectures that connected HR, Finance, Security and IT into a single governed platform.

I bring strategic clarity to complex technical challenges, a proven ability to lead without being directed and a commitment to building solutions that the business can trust and auditors can verify.
